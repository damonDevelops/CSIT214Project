Flight Cancellation

FlyDream Air allows customers to cnacel their flights up t 48 hours
prior to departure with no fees, however once cancelled, any seating
tickets are immediately un-reserved, and we cannot assure that they
will be available should you change your mind. Once you confirm that
you wish to cancel your flight, this action will be irreversible. Should
you change your mind, you may book your flight again through our
flight booking system, however we cannot assure availability of flights.
